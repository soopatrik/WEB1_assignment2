Our Story and Vision

Discover the journey and aspirations of VIAPets

Our Story

VIA-PETS was founded over a decade ago by Mr. Bob, VIAPets began as a small, passionate pet shop dedicated to matching pets with loving families. Mr. Bob’s vision was to create a welcoming space where people could experience the joy and companionship that pets bring to life. Starting with just a handful of animals, the shop quickly grew as word spread about the care and dedication that went into every adoption.

As the community grew, so did VIAPets. To meet customer needs, we expanded our services to include a kennel, ensuring pets had a safe, comfortable place to stay when their families were away. Today, VIAPets stands as a thriving center for pet adoptions, care, and temporary housing, bringing joy to both pets and their future families. We remain committed to Mr. Bob's original vision: to provide a community-driven space where every pet can find a home filled with love.

Our Vision

We believe every pet deserves a loving home. At VIAPets, we strive to create a community where pets and people come together. Our vision is to continue expanding, innovating, and offering the best possible experience for pets and pet owners alike.

  • Safe Temporary Care: Provide a secure, caring environment for pets when owners are unavailable.
  • Pet Well-being: Ensure pets are comfortable, happy, and healthy during their stay.
  • Customer Convenience: Offer a reliable solution for pet care needs, fostering trust and loyalty.
  • Support for Adoptions: Create a welcoming space that encourages potential adoptions by showing pets in a home-like setting.
