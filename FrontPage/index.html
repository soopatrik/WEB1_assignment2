Our Vision

At VIAPets, we believe every pet deserves a loving home. Mr. Bob's passion for animals drives our mission to connect wonderful pets with caring families.

With over 15 years of experience in pet care, we ensure each animal receives the attention and love they need until they find their forever home.

Our History

2010
The Beginning

Mr. Bob started VIAPets as a small local pet shop in London, driven by his passion for animal welfare and dream of connecting pets with loving families.

2015
International Expansion

After successful growth in the UK, VIAPets expanded to Slovakia, bringing our commitment to pet care and adoption to continental Europe.

2018
New Horizons

Further expansion into Moldova and Pakistan, establishing VIAPets as a global leader in pet adoption and care services.

2024
Today

Now with four international locations, we've helped over 10,000 pets find their forever homes while maintaining our commitment to exceptional care, looking to set our base in Denmark.

"From a small shop to an international family, our mission remains the same: giving every pet the loving home they deserve."

- Mr. Bob, Founder of VIAPets
